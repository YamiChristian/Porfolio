SmartNotes

Graphic Design
Project Overview
The objective of the project was to create an application that would allow students to become better at taking notes as well as helping to improve retention during study sessions to improve test scores.
My Contributions
I was in charge of research and development of this application. I gathered data based on interviews, surveys, as well as gathering information through competitor analaysis. I was also in charge of designing the app's user interface and user experience.

Looking at the current state of note-taking applications in the google play store, I have found a lot of mediocre applications that do not provide the quality service that students need to help them study. Through researching many different outlets, such as competitor applications, conducting user surveys and interviews, along with card sortings. I developed a Minimum Viable Product to align the users' needs with the client's visions.The result of this is a note-taking application that encourages students to study with A.I. generated practice exams and user-generated flashcards based on notes taken, which can be shared with users at a moment's notice.
Gathering Data
My first act on this project was to gather information. I gathered information by doing the following:
  • Conduct user surveys
  • Researched Android application and website
  • Used Personal experience using the services and applications for an expended period of time.

Personas

After gathering all of my data from my user interviews as well as my survey, I was able to organize my data into a few different sections including; pleasant points, pain points, items that help with retention, themes, and lastly key insights.

Problem

Students are in need an application that will allow them to take notes in a multitude of ways.A way to help improve retention of information when studying.An easy place to find accurate information for research projects and tests regarding subjects being taught. A single place where they can access their notes from any device.

Target User Base

The ideal users for our application will mostly focus on students towards a college or university education.Students at a high school/ secondary education level are also suitable candidates.

Minimum Viable Product

The application will include the following core features:

  • Note taking area with the ability to take handwritten notes
  • The ability to record audio/video
  • Document Scanner
  • Quiz Generator
  • Flash Card Deck

Solution

An application that will allow users to be able to purchase and use their tickets directly through their phones to get onto the bus. As well as letting them be able to find out where and when their bus will pick them up. It will also let them know about any delays or issues that may occur with their bus.
